Noodles

Description

This delicious ramen is made with 6 simple ingredients. This hearty bowl of noodles is perfect for any occasion. A cool summer night or a warm spring day, this bowl of ramen will fill you up and put a smile on your face. In less than 15 minutes you will be able to make the most delecatable bowl of ramen you will ever make. You can even make this for the whole family!

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Noodles
  • Soy Sauce
  • Green Onions
  • Beef Flank
  • Seaweed Wrap

Steps

  1. Buy noodles at the store (any kind will work but I prefer the more expensive brands).
  2. Boil the noodles with the included sauce packs in water until the noodles are al dente and transfer everything into a bowl.
  3. Soft boil an egg and after peeling, cut the egg in half and add them to the bowl of noodles.
  4. Chop a few green onions and add on top of the noodles.
  5. Buy some beef flank at the store and cut into small pieces it and cook it to add to the noodles.
  6. Add a crunchy seaweed wrap and slide it to the side of the bowl.
  7. Add soy sauce to your liking.
  8. Eat and enjoy!
